# 💡 Business Insights & Recommendations

A deeper breakdown of findings from the analysis.

---

## 🏆 Top Performing Areas
- **Electronics** is the clear revenue leader at **$11.1M** — 2.5× higher than the next category
- **Istanbul** dominates city-level performance with **$5.6M** in net revenue
- **Mobile devices** drive over half of all transactions and revenue

---

## ⚠️ Underperforming Areas
- **Books & Food** generate only ~$0.4M each — lowest of all categories
- **Eskisehir, Kayseri, Konya** are bottom-tier cities by revenue
- **29.45% of orders experience slow delivery (8–14 days)**

---

## 🎯 Strategic Recommendations

### Marketing
- Run dedicated campaigns for Electronics during Q4
- Increase customer acquisition spend in top 3 cities
- Test re-engagement campaigns in low-performing cities

### Operations
- Reduce delivery times by partnering with faster logistics providers
- Address the 2.79% logistics inefficiency segment (15–30 days delivery)

### Product Strategy
- Investigate Books & Food performance — pricing, range, marketing fit
- Bundle Electronics with high-margin accessories

### Customer Experience
- Improve mobile checkout UX since mobile generates 56% of orders
- Strengthen loyalty programs to grow beyond 60% returning customer base
- Push promotions in Credit & Debit card payments via cashback rewards

### Discount Strategy
- Re-evaluate whether heavy discounts (21%+, used in 28% of cases) are profit-positive
- Test smaller, smarter discount tiers to protect margin
